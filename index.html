OUR STORY

The story of GOD SOLDIERS TEAM GLOBAL begins in 2009. Daniel was a young missionary in the southern part of India. His ministry there was challenging. He recalled an occasion when he walked an entire day to preach in a certain village, only to find five people in attendance at his meeting—and none of them were saved.

It was a frustrating and discouraging time for him. Daniel cried out to the Lord, saying, "Oh God, this cannot be. This cannot be the impact of the glorious gospel." Then something happened that changed Daniel's life and the course of history.

OUR STORY

The story of GOD SOLDIERS TEAM GLOBAL begins in 2009. Daniel was a young missionary in the southern part of India. His ministry there was challenging. He recalled an occasion when he walked an entire day to preach in a certain village, only to find five people in attendance at his meeting—and none of them were saved.

It was a frustrating and discouraging time for him. Daniel cried out to the Lord, saying, "Oh God, this cannot be. This cannot be the impact of the glorious gospel." Then something happened that changed Daniel's life and the course of history.

APOSTOLIC VISION

VISION & MISSIONS

"I left the 99 and ran after the one—the lost one is you, Daniel. I have saved you, and in the same way, you must save the lost souls."

One night, he had a dream where he heard the voice of the Holy Spirit saying, "The world SHALL be saved." That dream marked the beginning of the ministry called GOD SOLDIERS TEAM GLOBAL. Today, we witness over 1,000 people make a decision for Christ in single service, and up to 3,000 over the course of a meeting.

Our Founder

K.Daniel is the founder of GOD SOLDIERS TEAM GLOBAL. He is an evangelist who has spent most of his ministry preaching the gospel in more than 25 states in india and also globally. As a missionary, Daniel has led thousands of people to Christ face-to-face through open-air evangelistic campaigns in some of the most dangerous, difficult, and remote locations on earth.

“God is working to create a community of redeemed people from every tribe, tongue and nation for his glory. This will help you better understand God's mission (missio-Dei) and the vital role of Christ Followers is called to play in advancing his kingdom.”
